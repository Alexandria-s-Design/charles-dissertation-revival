# 12-Month Roadmap

## Objective

Turn the dissertation into four durable assets:

1. academic credibility
2. public visibility
3. a sellable offer
4. a reusable framework

## Quarter 1: Foundation

- finalize the core positioning
- create the named framework
- start the flagship article
- update Charles's public-facing positioning
- publish initial public content

Success markers:

- framework named and documented
- article outline complete
- three target journals identified
- public positioning language updated
- five content pieces published

## Quarter 2: Credibility

- finish and submit the lead article
- reconnect with academic and practitioner networks
- develop conference and speaking proposals
- create one authority asset such as a white paper

Success markers:

- first article submitted
- one or two speaking proposals drafted or sent
- one white paper completed
- additional public pieces published
- several strategic reconnections made

## Quarter 3: Monetization

- define one flagship service offer
- productize one asset
- build sales materials
- prospect intentionally into schools, districts, and language-focused organizations

Success markers:

- one offer fully defined
- one productized asset ready
- sales materials complete
- first qualified prospects identified
- first consulting or workshop conversations started

## Quarter 4: Expansion

- build the second publication
- expand the speaking pipeline
- strengthen brand architecture
- evaluate traction and set year-two strategy

Success markers:

- second major manuscript or article drafted
- two or three speaking opportunities secured or pending
- one paid or pilot engagement completed
- framework embedded into Charles's brand
- year-two strategy ready

## Priority Sequence

1. framework
2. lead article
3. public positioning
4. workshop offer
5. toolkit or product
6. speaking pipeline
